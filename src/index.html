MY LIFE JOURNEY

Age 12 - Computer? Love at first sight!

One day, a glowing computer showed up at our home. While everyone else was excited to play games, I was the curious one asking, "How does this magic box even work?" The moment I discovered websites were built with "code," it felt like I unlocked a secret door to another world.

Age 15 – Building websites? Like doing magic tricks!

I met HTML and CSS and instantly became a fan. Centering a button on the page? That was my masterpiece! Every tiny change felt like a superpower. That’s when I realized — code runs in my veins

Age 19 - Enter JavaScript!

Things started getting serious. JavaScript walked in, and boom — the web got interactive! The first time I triggered an alert() message, I legit felt like a hacker

Age 21 - Vue.js: My coding soulmate

This was when I got introduced to Vue.js, and oh boy — it changed everything. I finally learned how to write clean, organized, and scalable code. My projects became more complex, but I loved the challenge.

Age 22 – Three.js: A 3D love story

One day someone asked me, “Have you ever tried Three.js?” I said no. I tried it... and never looked back. Suddenly, I was building mountains, adding lights, and even launching spaceships — all inside the browser. Okay, they weren’t real spaceships, but still... it was AWESOME!

Today – Still Simple Programmer

Looking back, I’m still that curious kid at heart — just with a few more programming languages in my toolbox. I still get excited over a cool animation or a clean UI. And yes, when my code breaks, I grab a cup of tea, take a deep breath… and dive right back in.
